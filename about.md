I've been with the same company for nearly 30 years — the last 25+ of them
as its systems administrator, running Windows and Active Directory
infrastructure. Offensive security is my craft outside of work: I study it
seriously, mostly through Hack The Box, and I hold the **HTB Certified
Penetration Testing Specialist (CPTS)** certification.

I keep infrastructure running for a living and spend my own time learning
how it gets broken into — the same systems, seen from the other side.

This site collects my penetration testing writeups (retired lab machines
only), cheatsheets I rely on in practice, and shorter notes on methodology.

My focus is Active Directory attack chains — ACL abuse, DPAPI,
BloodHound-driven path analysis, post-exploitation — and, drawing on the
operational background, the defensive flip side of every technique.
